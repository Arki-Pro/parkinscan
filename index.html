ParkScreen

1. Project Core

The Problem: 5.8 million people in India have Parkinson's disease, but most live far away from specialist brain doctors. By the time they get diagnosed, the disease is already in advanced stages.

The Solution: A ₹1,500 smart wearable device. It acts like a digital health detective that listens to a person's voice and measures their hand tremors to catch Parkinson's early, right in their own village.

3. The 22 Biomarkers (Clues it looks for)

Instead of guessing, the device tracks exactly 22 clinical signs:

  • Voice Clues (9): It listens for hoarseness, sudden pitch drops, and breathing pauses that the human ear can't catch.
  • Movement Clues (8): It measures the exact speed of hand tremors, muscle stiffness, and how a person walks (gait).
  • System Rules (5): Built-in speed limits to ensure the device runs fast and doesn't drain the battery.

4. The Brain (ML Architecture)

We use a Dual-Model Ensemble. Think of it like a hospital where you get two doctors to look at your test results instead of one:

  • Model 1: Uses simple logic that is easy for human doctors to read and understand.
  • Model 2: Uses a complex math boundary to get super accurate results (about 91% accurate in tests).

The Formula: The device blends both opinions to give a final "Risk Score" out of 100%.

5. Smart System Features

  • Quality Check: If it's too noisy in the room, it rejects the recording instead of giving a wrong answer.
  • Honesty Meter: If the device is confused, it will say "Low Confidence, please test again" instead of guessing.
  • No Secrets: It tells the doctor exactly *why* it gave a high score (e.g., "Hand tremor was too fast").
  • Memory: It remembers the patient's scores over weeks to see if they are getting worse or better.

10. Training Data

Right now, the AI is trained on data from Oxford University. For Round 2, we will train it on Indian patient data to make it perfectly tuned for our local population.

11. Handling Anxiety & Colds

What if a patient is just nervous or has a bad cough? The device won't be fooled.

  • Audio Filters: It strips out sounds like throat-clearing automatically before checking the voice.
  • Time is the Truth: Anxiety goes away in a week, Parkinson's doesn't. If the device detects strange numbers, it tells the patient to come back next week. If it was just nerves, the score will drop to normal.
